XOR CIPHER

This cipher works similarly to the Vigenere cipher It works by taking the XOR operation of each bit of the key and the message and putting that into the ciphertext. Encryption works exactly the same as decryption, since A XOR B XOR A = B. Shorter key lengths make the cipher vulnerable to a possible cryptanalysis attack. Keys with a length greater than or equal to the length of the message offer perfect secrecy, where the ciphertext does not give any information about the plaintext or key.
